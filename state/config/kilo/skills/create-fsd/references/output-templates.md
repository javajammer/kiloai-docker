# Output Templates by Review Level

## Tech Lead Template

Use when: reviewing technical depth, architecture quality,
security implementation, code-level concerns.

Focus areas:
- Design pattern appropriateness
- Security threat analysis
- Performance bottleneck identification
- Integration risk assessment
- Technical debt evaluation
- Scalability concerns
- Technology choice validation

Output emphasis: Specific, actionable technical findings with
code/config references where applicable. Include severity ratings.

## VP Template

Use when: need helicopter view with enough technical depth
to make informed decisions and delegate effectively.

Focus areas:
- Business alignment (are we building the right thing?)
- Resource adequacy (do we have the people/budget?)
- Timeline realism (can we deliver on time?)
- Risk management (what keeps me up at night?)
- Cross-team coordination (what blocks us?)
- Vendor/cloud cost (are we spending wisely?)
- Compliance readiness (are we audit-ready?)

Output emphasis: Executive summary first, then drill-down by area.
Top 3 actions. Risk matrix. Go/No-Go with conditions.

## Board of Directors Template

Use when: presenting to non-technical leadership for
strategic decisions, budget approval, or go/no-go.

Focus areas:
- ROI and business case (what's the return?)
- Competitive impact (does this differentiate us?)
- Regulatory risk (are we compliant?)
- Organizational change (what changes for our people?)
- Long-term sustainability (3-5 year outlook)
- Investment justification (why now? why this amount?)

Output emphasis: Non-technical language. Clear recommendation with conditions. Risk summary.
